Rosebank College is a private college in South Africa that is a member of The Independent Institute of Education (IIE).
It provides a variety of IIE-accredited degrees, diplomas, and postgraduate degrees in subjects like law, education, commerce, and information technology.
In order to educate students for the competitive job market and to enable graduates to become future leaders, the college offers flexible learning alternatives such full-time, blended, and distance (online) learning.

Rosebank College offers other funding options through partners like Student Hero, which provides information on various funding opportunities and because it is a private institute it doesnt offer NSFAS.
